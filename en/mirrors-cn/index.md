# Open-Source Software Mirror Sites (China)


A collection of open-source software mirror sites in China. The recommended ones are preferred. You can also browse the [public open-source mirrors](https://lug.org.cn/doku.php?id=china-public-open-source-mirrors) or the [education-network open-source mirrors](https://lug.org.cn/doku.php?id=china-edu-open-source-mirrors) directly.
